Digital delivery

Templates are digital products. Refunds are limited once files or access have been delivered or downloaded.

03

Non-refundable cases

Refunds are usually not available for change of mind, wrong license choice, lack of required software, or requests for features not listed on the product page.

If something is not working as described, contact us first. We will review the issue and try to provide a fix or updated file.
